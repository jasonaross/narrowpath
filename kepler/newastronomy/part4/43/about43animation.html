About the animation

This animation is made with the true eccentricities of the vicarious hypothesis, and the eccentricity used by Kepler in chapter 43 for area-time. Since the differences between the locations are small (around 8' max), the differences between the three different locations have been exaggerated. This means that the bisected and the area-time locations have had their distance from the vicarious location multiplied by an exaggeration factor (25× in this animation).
